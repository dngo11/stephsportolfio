User Testing

prototype: observing how and why users interact with early iterations of a product or service

Greater Than One

wanted to test a new concept related to genomics testing for cancer patients. The goal was the assess genomics health literacy among patients and caregivers. I created a descriptive concept and sent it via e-mail to cancer patients and caregivers. While I was on the phone with users, they read and responded to the concept so we could capture in-real-time feedback.

Outcome

Concept testing with 18 patients and caregivers resulted in three iterations. Preliminary responses included "What is genomic testing? I don’t understand" and "Can you explain this to me?" By the end of testing, overall user response was "“The fact that you can find out about your type of cancer on a molecular level makes so much sense. It is so important" and “If this existed then we wouldn’t have to troll the entire Internet for what you needed. It's so useful.”
