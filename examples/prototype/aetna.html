User Testing

prototype: observing how and why users interact with early iterations of a product or service

Aetna, Inc.

was developing new digital price transparency healthcare tool for The Home Depot employees. I assembled and led the research team over the course of six months. After conducting store manager interviews, an online survey, and employee phone interviews, we went to stores around the country to test a prototype with 23 employees. Based on user feedback, iterations to the prototype were made during testing.

Outcome

in-store testing with 23 employees to use real time feedback to make product iterations.
