Behavior Design

define: identifying essential qualities of product and program design

Industry Workshops

Inspired by scientifically validated health behavior change theories (such at the Fogg Behavior Model, Health Belief Model, Social Cognitive Theory, etc), this 3-hour interactive workshop enables teams to approach behavioral outcomes in a new and unified way. Behavior Design is an effective behavior change methodology birthed and cultivated at Stanford University – it is a blend of best practices from behavioral science and experience design. It is a systematic way of thinking about behavior change; driven by psychological research to ensure we match target behaviors with solutions for achieving those behaviors.
