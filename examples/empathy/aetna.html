User Research
(Empathy)

Aetna, Inc.

was developing new digital price transparency healthcare tool for Nordstrom employees. The goal was to make the tool "just right for Nordstrom, and that meant "simple and sticky.” Over the course of three months, I assembled and led the research team to establish a clear understanding of users healthcare experiences, enabling us to respond to “sticky” and “unique” We conducted phone and in-person interviews with 74 employees across the country to translate needs and motivations directly into the product design.
