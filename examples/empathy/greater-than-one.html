Greater Than One

was helping a client develop a new cancer patient portal. The goal was to pinpoint a way to "create utility for cancer patients to better navigate their treatment decisions." I designed and conducted research that entailed 20 phone interviews with cancer patients and caregivers across the country over the course of six weeks; then translated needs and motivations into a patient journey map.
